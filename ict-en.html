Enterprise Project Management Office (EPMO) Accessibility Requirements

In order to check if accessibility is needed, the IT Accessibility Office (ITAO) uses the ICT Accessibility Requirements Wizard tool. This is a preliminary tool that must be completed to ascertain if accessibility is required for your project. This tool goal is to help identify potential accessibility requirements and acts as a point of first contact for managers with the IT Accessibility Office (ITAO).

Instructions

To best utilize this tool, follow each step in order. Please note that you may need advice from the IT team that is developing your project while completing the tool.

  1. Review Supporting Information section related to accessibility standards and policies.
  2. Open the ICT Accessibility Requirements Wizard.
  3. Complete Step 1: Define ICT functionality to the best of your knowledge by selecting all checkboxes that apply to the ICT being procured.
  4. Skip Step 2: Refine close selection.
  5. Go to Step 3: Generate requirements document, click the first link Full requirements table with informative sections (English) and save document.
  6. Send the ICT Accessibility Requirements document generated at Step 5 to theIT Accessibility Office.
  7. Contact the IT Accessibility Office to register for an accessibility coaching session if required.
